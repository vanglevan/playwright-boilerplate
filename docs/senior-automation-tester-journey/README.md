# 🎓 Senior Automation Tester 2026 — Post-Bootcamp Curriculum

> **Prerequisite:** Hoàn thành [30-day bootcamp](../playwright-learning-journey/README.md) với 4 tuần cơ bản.
> **Duration:** 6 months (part-time, ~8-12h/week) hoặc 3 months intensive (20h/week)
> **Goal:** Từ mid-level automation tester → senior với AI-native mindset, ready cho 2026 job market.

---

## 🎯 Tại sao curriculum này tồn tại

Năm **2026 là năm AI đang định nghĩa lại automation testing**:

- 60-80% code test được AI-assist hoặc AI-generate
- Job descriptions **mention AI skills** ở >70% senior roles
- Companies trả **+20-40%** cho tester biết AI-native workflows
- Senior position expect **agentic thinking**, không chỉ "viết test"

**Tester không adapt AI → sẽ bị junior AI-native thay thế trong 2-3 năm.**

30-day bootcamp đã cho bạn foundation. Curriculum này nâng cấp bạn thành **thinker, architect, và AI-native engineer** — skillset của senior 2026.

---

## 🏛️ 4 Trụ cột

### 1. AI Mastery (50% focus)

Phần khó nhất nhưng **giá trị cao nhất** 2026. Không chỉ "dùng AI", mà:

- Hiểu AI hoạt động như thế nào (conceptual)
- Prompt engineering ở cấp độ patterns
- Agentic engineering (build agents, not use them)
- Apply AI vào mọi khâu QA workflow
- Best practices + ethics + safety

### 2. Technical Depth (25% focus)

Beyond E2E — senior tester phải thành thạo multiple dimensions:

- Performance testing (k6, JMeter, Gatling)
- Security testing (OWASP, ZAP, penetration basics)
- Mobile testing (Appium, Maestro, Detox)
- Contract testing (Pact)
- Chaos engineering
- Observability (logs, metrics, traces)
- System design cơ bản cho distributed systems

### 3. Strategy & Leadership (15% focus)

Senior không chỉ write tests — senior **design strategy** và **lead people**:

- Test strategy architecture
- Soft skills (communication, stakeholder mgmt)
- Code review culture, mentorship
- Cross-functional collaboration

### 4. Business & Career (10% focus)

Senior hiểu **why**, không chỉ **how**:

- ROI measurement cho QA work
- Testing metrics (DORA, SPACE)
- Senior interview patterns
- Path to Staff/Principal
- Compensation benchmarking

## 🧭 Learning principles

### 1. **Depth > Breadth (initially)**

Pick 1 AI tool (Claude Code), master it 3 months. Don't surface-skim 5 tools.

### 2. **Ship > Study**

Every week: build something. Study ≠ learning; building = learning.

### 3. **Teach to learn**

Blog, share on LinkedIn, mentor juniors. You truly know only what you can explain.

### 4. **Measure productivity**

Track AI impact weekly. Adjust. Drop workflows that don't net-help.

### 5. **Community over cave**

Join Playwright Discord, automation communities. Senior mindset = collaborative.

### 6. **Specialize at 6 months**

After foundation, pick 1 direction:

- AI-native testing engineer
- Performance specialist
- Security testing specialist
- Test architect / strategist
- Mobile testing specialist

## ✅ Success criteria (end of 6 months)

### Technical

- [ ] Build + deploy 1 custom AI agent for testing
- [ ] Lead test strategy for 1 real project (could be personal)
- [ ] 3+ test suites: performance, security, mobile
- [ ] 200+ GitHub commits ongoing activity
- [ ] Portfolio repo 50+ stars (or 500+ followers LinkedIn)

### Career

- [ ] 5+ interviews completed
- [ ] 1-2 offers at senior level
- [ ] 3 blog posts / conference talk proposal
- [ ] 1 OSS contribution to notable project

### AI fluency

- [ ] Operate Claude Code/Cursor at expert level
- [ ] Can design + build simple agents
- [ ] Can explain LLM behavior to teammates
- [ ] `AI_WORKFLOW.md` adopted by team

## 🧰 Time management suggestion

### Part-time (6 months, 8-12h/week)

- 4h: Reading + tutorials
- 4h: Building (code)
- 2h: Reflect + document
- 1-2h: Community / content creation

### Intensive (3 months, 20h/week)

Week split:

- Mon-Fri: 3h/day focused work
- Sat: 5h deep project
- Sun: 2h reflect + plan

### Weekend warriors (12 months, 4-6h/week)

Realistic nếu fulltime busy. Progress slower but sustainable.

## 🚨 When to skip curriculum parts

This is advisory, not gospel.

**Skip or defer:**

- Mobile testing section if you don't work on mobile apps
- Chaos engineering if company isn't at that scale
- Certain AI sections if you don't use those tools

**Never skip:**

- Prompt engineering (universal)
- Best practices (safety)
- Test strategy (required for senior)
- Soft skills (career blockers)

## 🌟 Final note

**Senior không phải title — là mindset.**

Bạn là senior khi:

- Giải quyết ambiguity (không chỉ well-defined tasks)
- Dạy người khác và lift team
- Think 3 months ahead, không chỉ this sprint
- Know when NOT to automate (huge)
- Handle failure gracefully + learn
- Optimize for company outcome, không chỉ code quality

**Curriculum này là syllabus cho mindset đó.**
